The Bank Account Management System is a secure and efficient software designed to manage customer accounts and banking operations. It helps banks and users perform essential tasks like account creation, balance check, deposit, withdrawal, fund transfer, and transaction history with ease and accuracy.

✔ Account Creation

Create new customer accounts with details such as name, age, address, account type, and initial balance.

✔ Deposit & Withdrawal

Easily add or withdraw money from accounts while updating the balance instantly.

✔ Balance Inquiry

View the current balance of any account at any time.

✔ Fund Transfer

Transfer money securely between two accounts within the system.

✔ Transaction History

Stores a complete record of all transactions for future reference.

✔ Account Management

Update account details, delete accounts, or view customer information.

✔ Secure Login

Admin login/user login with password protection (optional, based on your project).
